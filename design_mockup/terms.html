1. Service Description

The D2D Ordering System is a specialized interface designed for tracking order status and providing service feedback. This platform does not facilitate direct order placement by customers.

2. User Conduct

Users may access the system to monitor active fulfillment requests. Unauthorized attempts to access administrative functions or the Staff Portal are strictly prohibited.

3. Order Tracking

All orders are initialized by authorized fulfillment center staff. The system provides real-time updates as the order transitions from PENDING to READY and COLLECTED. D2D is a monitoring tool and is not responsible for the contents or timing of the orders.

4. Feedback Submission

Users are encouraged to share their service experience through the feedback module. This data is used to optimize fulfillment intelligence and service quality.

5. Data Privacy

We process minimal user data (primarily email identifiers) solely for the purpose of matching users with their active fulfillment requests.
